# how-to-employee-survey-on-identity-dimensions
**Intersectionality survey** assessing demographic and identity dimensions typically subject to discrimination or privilege. *This repository is work-in-progress.*

The survey assesses: age range, highest educational degree of guardian(s) during youth, home countries of last two generations within the family, citizenship, religious practice (at work), and gender, biological sex and sexual orientation.
